Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to:

  1. Explore current issues in biology
  2. Analyze current issues in biology
  3. Analyze personal paradigms related to ethical issues in biology
  4. Write a scholarly analytical paper on an ethical issue in biology
  5. Examine data that support various positions on ethical issues in biology
