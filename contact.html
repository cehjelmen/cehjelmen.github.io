How to Request a Meeting

  1. Check the calendar → Look at the calendar on this page for a time that appears open for both of us.
  2. Send an email Write to Carl.Hjelmen@uvu.edu or cehjelmen@gmail.com with your proposed date, time, and a brief note about what you'd like to discuss.
  3. Wait for confirmation I'll reply to confirm or suggest an alternative time. The meeting is not scheduled until you receive a confirmation.
There is no automated booking system. All meeting requests must go through email — do not assume a time is reserved until you hear back from me directly.
If the calendar shows "Office Hours", you are welcome to stop by SB 242b without an appointment during that time.
